About Us


We are a team of software developers and researchers. Our main goal is to help tea farmers detect tea leaf diseases early, check the health status of leaves, and find sustainable solutions. We know tea farming is a big part of Sri Lanka’s economy. Early detection can save crops, reduce losses, and help farmers earn more.


We are building a smart system. This system can detect if a tea leaf is healthy or infected. It uses artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning. By looking at the image of a tea leaf, the system will detect diseases such as white spot, bird eye spot, or brown blight. This helps farmers take action before it’s too late.


In many places, tea farmers don’t have access to expensive tools. But this system will be simple and easy to use. We hope to give them a free and fast way to check their leaves. This tool will make a big difference in the lives of tea farmers.
