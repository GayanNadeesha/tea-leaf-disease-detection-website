Tea Leaf Disease Prediction Result

Disease Detected: Bird Eye Spot (Cercospora theae)

Reason: Fungal infection (Cercospora theae)

Appearance: Small, round black or dark brown spots with light centers (1-5mm diameter)

Type of Damage:

  • Reduces photosynthesis by damaging leaf surface
  • Weakens the plant over time
  • Lowers both tea quality and yield

Can Be Cured: Yes

Spread: Thrives in humid conditions, spreads via rain splash

Solutions For Disease

  • Remove and destroy infected leaves
  • Apply copper-based fungicides
  • Improve air circulation through pruning
  • Avoid overhead irrigation
